如有以下任一情況，請立即致電 999：

  • ❤️ 心臟病發作的跡象 (Signs of a heart attack)：
    胸痛、心口痛、感到壓力擠壓胸部、有心臟病病史
    Chest pain, pressure, heaviness, tightness or squeezing across the chest
  • 🧠 中風的跡象 (Signs of a stroke)：
    臉一側下垂，雙臂無法舉起，說話困難／模糊
    Face drooping on one side, can’t hold both arms up, difficulty speaking
  • 😵‍💫 突然意識混亂 (Sudden confusion, delirium)：
    無法確定自己的姓名或年齡
    Cannot be sure of own name or age
  • 🆘 自殺未遂或有自殘傾向 (Suicide attempt)：
    嘗試自殺或自我傷害
    By taking something or self-harming
  • 😮‍💨 嚴重呼吸困難 (Severe difficulty breathing)：
    無法說話，窒息或喘氣
    Not being able to get words out, choking or gasping
  • 🩸 大量出血 (Heavy bleeding)：
    噴灑或出血量足以形成水坑
    Spraying, pouring or enough to make a puddle
  • 💥 嚴重創傷 (Severe injuries)：
    發生嚴重事故後
    After a serious accident
  • ⚡ 癲癇發作 (Seizure, fit)：
    因痙攣發抖、失去知覺（無法醒來）
    Shaking or jerking because of a fit, or unconscious (can’t be woken up)
  • 🤧 嚴重過敏反應 (Sudden, rapid swelling)：
    嘴唇、嘴巴、喉嚨或舌頭腫脹
    Of the lips, mouth, throat or tongue
  • 👶 臨產或分娩 (Labour or childbirth)：
    破水、更頻繁劇烈宮縮、嬰兒即將出生或剛出生
    Waters breaking, more frequent intense cramps (contractions), baby coming, or just born
  • 🧱 頭部受傷 (Head injury)：
    視力模糊、嘔吐、暈眩或失去知覺
    Blurred vision, vomiting, dizziness or unconsciousness after head trauma
  • 🔥 大面積燒燙傷 (Large-area burns)：
    嚴重或範圍大的燒傷
    Severe or widespread burns
沒有以上情況 None Of Above
